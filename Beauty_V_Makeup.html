Beauty

Beauty is a subjective quality that can be difficult to define. It can be a property of things that are nice to look at, hear, feel, taste, smell, or think about. Beauty can also refer to a feeling that varies from person to person and culture to culture.

Makeup

Makeup can stimulate the senses of touch, smell, and sight, which can lead to psychological and sensory pleasure. It can also be used to accentuate natural features, such as eyes and lips, and give skin a smooth appearance.
